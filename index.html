biography

own is a graphic designer from san francisco bay area. her calculated and multifaceted approach to graphic design has landed her work with musicians such as olswel, mescaline maniacs, rainsdeaf as well as designing several successful e-commerce businesses. early on she focused mainly on incorporating fine art and mixed mdeia into comissioned artwork. being a touring musician herself has heavily influenced her work, inspiration, and her drive to help others turn their ideas into polished bodies of work. today she continues her craft as the "go to" artist for independent bands to bring their ideas to fruition, as well as balancing being an efficient website developer, product manager, and audio engineer.
